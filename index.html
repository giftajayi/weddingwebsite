Our Story

Our story began on a beautiful and unexpected evening in 2013 at Obafemi Awolowo University. After a long night of studying during a campus power outage, Gbemi was ready to return to her hostel but hesitated to walk back alone. She decided to wait until someone else was leaving the classroom so they could walk together. That was when Funto was leaving. A simple introduction and a request to walk together turned into a conversation that lasted over an hour. We talked about our studies, interests, dreams, and aspirations. What started as a simple walk became the foundation of a friendship that, years later, blossomed into a loving relationship. Today, we are grateful for the journey that brought us here and excited to begin our forever together.
