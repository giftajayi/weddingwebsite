Our Story

Our story began on a beautiful and unexpected evening in 2013 at Obafemi Awolowo University. After a long night of studying during a campus power outage, I was ready to return to my hostel but hesitated to walk back alone. I decided to wait until someone else was leaving the classroom so we could walk together. That was when Funto was leaving. I walked up to him, introduced myself, and asked if he would mind walking back with me. He gladly agreed, and what started as a simple walk turned into a conversation that lasted for over an hour. We talked about our studies, our interests, and our dreams for the future. From that first conversation, a beautiful friendship began. What started as a simple walk became the foundation of a friendship that, years later, blossomed into love. Today, we are grateful for the journey that brought us here and excited to begin our forever together.
