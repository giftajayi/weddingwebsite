Our Story

Our story began on a beautiful and unexpected evening in 2013 at Obafemi Awolowo University. After a long night of studying during a campus power outage, Gbemi was ready to return to her hostel but hesitated to walk back alone. She decided to wait until someone else was leaving the classroom so they could walk together. That was when Funto was leaving. A simple introduction and a request to walk together turned into a conversation that lasted over an hour. They talked about their studies, interests, dreams, and aspirations. From that first conversation, a beautiful friendship began. Throughout their university years, they remained close friends, supporting and encouraging each other through different seasons of life. What started as a simple walk became the foundation of a friendship that, years later, blossomed into love. Today, they are grateful for the journey that brought them here and excited to begin their forever together.
